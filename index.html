Machine Learning Operations

NYC Vision Zero MLOps

The Situation

Vision Zero hopes to reduce the amount of general vehicular related incidents. To support this, there was a need to evaluate historical and streaming data to determine risk.

The Action

Created an MLOps pipeline to classify the risk of possible incidents on any given day. Engineered a RandomForestClassifier model integrated with Kubernetes, MLFlow, and a Ray Cluster, served via a FastAPI REST endpoint.

The Impact

Equipped groups like the NYPD and NYCDOT to better predict potential incidents based on existing daily conditions, ultimately aiming to enable faster response times and safer roads.

Predictive Modeling & Strategy

US Airline Flight Delay Analysis

The Situation

With 20% of U.S. flights arriving late, airlines face $30B in annual losses due to crew overtime, repositioning, and missed connections.

The Action

Processed over 5 million flight records to build a predictive model evaluating the likelihood of a flight being delayed by more than 15 minutes, utilizing a Decision Tree classifier.

The Impact

The model achieved a lift above 3.7 in the top 10% of predicted delay risk flights, allowing operations teams to proactively optimize aircraft and crew allocation.
